Banana Walnut Cake

A delicious banana cake generously topped with walnuts

Italian

Active time: 24 min
Total time: 60 min
Serves: 10 people

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. bananas
  • 1 lb. walnuts
  • 1 gallon olive oil
  • 1 lb. spinach
  • 2 boxes of baking soda
  • 1 gallon water

Steps

  1. Mix everything together in a 1 oz bowl
  2. Lick the batter off the spoon
  3. Put it in the oven at 500° F for 2 hours
  4. Check it with a skewer to check that it is thoroughly burnt
  5. Eat the entire thing
  6. Pack up your bags and book a plane ticket to Hawaii
  7. Eat some good food there
