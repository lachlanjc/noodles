What's Noodles?


No, Noodles isn't that Japanese food you ate in college. It's an app where you can straighten out your recipes.
Remember that delicious pie your mom made? Remember where the recipe is for it? Thought so. Noodles lets you take a picture of your recipe and with a SNAP, the recipe is made into text and sent to our servers and Evernote. So you can relive childhood, one bite at a time.
